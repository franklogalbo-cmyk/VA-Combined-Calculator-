VA Combined Rating Calculator

Refined for 38 CFR § 4.26. This version tests multiple bilateral-factor scenarios and automatically selects the most favorable outcome.

How to use:
Enter ratings as comma-separated values, such as 30, 20, 10.
  • Left / Right Upper Extremity: arms, shoulders, elbows, wrists, hands
  • Left / Right Lower Extremity: hips, thighs, knees, ankles, feet
  • Other Ratings: all remaining non-extremity or non-bilateral ratings
Method used here:
1. Determine whether the upper pair and/or lower pair are bilateral-factor eligible.
2. Build every valid bilateral scenario.
3. For each scenario, combine included bilateral extremity ratings in severity order.
4. Add 10% of that combined extremity value.
5. Combine excluded extremity ratings and other ratings separately.
6. Choose the final result most favorable to the veteran.
