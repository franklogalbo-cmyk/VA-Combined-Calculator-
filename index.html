VA Combined Rating Calculator

This version supports named conditions, shows the combined bilateral factor for upper and lower extremities, and tests valid § 4.26(d) exclusion scenarios to select the most favorable result.

How to use:
Enter one row per condition. Choose the body area: Left Upper Right Upper Left Lower Right Lower Other

Use the Bilateral Eligible checkbox only for conditions that should be included in the bilateral-factor analysis. Conditions marked Other are always combined outside the bilateral-factor pool.
Example: Left knee strain 10% → Left Lower. Right knee instability 20% → Right Lower.
38 C.F.R. § 4.26 logic modeled here:
1. Bilateral factor requires compensable disability in each of two paired extremities.
2. If both upper and lower extremities are included, the 4 extremities are combined together first, then one 10% bilateral factor is added.
3. Under § 4.26(d), if excluding one or more bilateral disabilities produces a better overall result, those excluded disabilities are combined separately and the most favorable outcome is selected.
