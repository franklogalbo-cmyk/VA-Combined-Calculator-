VA Combined Rating Calculator

Refined to follow 38 CFR § 4.26 more closely. When both upper and lower paired extremities are involved, all eligible extremity ratings are combined together first, then one bilateral factor is added.

How to use:
Enter ratings as comma-separated values, such as 30, 20, 10.
  • Left / Right Upper Extremity: arms, shoulders, elbows, wrists, hands
  • Left / Right Lower Extremity: hips, thighs, knees, ankles, feet
  • Other Ratings: all remaining non-extremity or non-bilateral ratings
38 CFR § 4.26 approach used here:
1. Identify compensable disabilities of paired extremities.
2. Combine eligible bilateral extremity ratings in order of severity.
3. Add 10% of that combined value to the extremity combined value.
4. Treat that result as one disability for further combination with other ratings.
5. Show a comparison to a non-bilateral approach to help identify the more favorable result.
