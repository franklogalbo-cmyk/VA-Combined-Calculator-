VA Math Reminder
VA math does not add ratings in a simple way. Ratings are combined from highest to lowest using remaining efficiency:

New Combined = Current Combined + (100 - Current Combined) × Next Rating

After bilateral factor adjustments, all ratings are combined and the final number is rounded to the nearest 10%.
