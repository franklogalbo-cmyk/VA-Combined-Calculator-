VA Combined Rating Calculator

This version supports named conditions, shows the bilateral factor for the combined upper and lower extremities, and includes a visible validation section for § 4.26(d) so the user can see why a scenario was selected.

How to use:
Enter one row per condition. Choose the body area: Left UpperRight UpperLeft LowerRight LowerOther

Use the Bilateral Eligible checkbox only for conditions that should be included in bilateral-factor analysis. Conditions marked Other are always combined outside the bilateral-factor pool.
Example: Left knee strain 10% → Left Lower. Right knee instability 20% → Right Lower.
Modeled logic:
1. The calculator identifies bilateral-pool extremity conditions.
2. It tests valid bilateral scenarios.
3. It shows the combined extremity value and the bilateral factor.
4. It validates whether exclusions improve the result under § 4.26(d).
5. It selects the most favorable scenario and shows why.
