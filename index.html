VA Combined Rating Calculator

This version shows the bilateral factor for the combined upper and lower extremities together, then combines that adjusted value with all other ratings.

How to use:
Enter ratings as comma-separated values such as 30, 20, 10.
Bilateral factor display:
If both upper and lower extremities qualify, all eligible extremity ratings are combined together first.
Then 10% of that combined extremity value is added as the bilateral factor.
The adjusted bilateral value is then treated as one disability and combined with all other ratings.
