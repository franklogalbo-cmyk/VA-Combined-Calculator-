Nexus Strategy & Advocacy Notes
Regulatory Reference — 38 CFR § 4.26
§ 4.25Whole-person combination. Rounding is two-step: decimal → whole number (≥ 0.5 rounds up) → nearest 10 (5 rounds up). Single-step rounding produces wrong results.
§ 4.26Combine bilateral conditions highest-to-lowest, then ADD (not combine) 10% of that combined value. Treat result as one disability for further combination.
§ 4.26(b)Four-extremity rule: both upper AND lower bilateral pairs combine in one group with one 10% bonus.
§ 4.26(c)Bilateral factor requires a compensable (≥ 10%) rating in each of the 2 paired extremities.
§ 4.26(d)Exception (eff. Apr 16 2023): VA must automatically exclude bilateral conditions from the group when doing so yields a higher rating. This calculator tests all 2ⁿ subsets to find the optimal configuration.
