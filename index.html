Nexus Strategy & Advocacy Notes
Regulatory Reference — 38 CFR § 4.26
§ 4.25 Whole-person combination. Rounding is a two-step process: decimal → whole number (≥ 0.5 rounds up), then → nearest 10 (values ending in 5 round up). Single-step rounding is incorrect and can produce wrong results.
§ 4.26 Combine bilateral conditions highest-to-lowest, then ADD (not combine) 10% of that combined value. Treat the result as one disability for further combination.
§ 4.26(b) Four-extremity rule: if both upper AND lower extremity bilateral pairs exist, combine all four in one group with one 10% bonus.
§ 4.26(c) Bilateral factor requires a compensable (≥ 10%) rating in each of the 2 paired extremities — not just one side.
§ 4.26(d) Exception (eff. Apr 16 2023, 88 FR 22917): VA must automatically exclude one or more bilateral conditions from the group when doing so yields a higher rating. This calculator tests all 2ⁿ subsets to find the optimal configuration.
