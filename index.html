VA Combined Rating Calculator

Refined to model 38 CFR § 4.26, including § 4.26(d). This version tests valid bilateral scenarios, shows when upper and lower extremities are combined together for one bilateral factor, and selects the most favorable result.

How to use:
Enter ratings as comma-separated values, such as 30, 20, 10.
  • Left / Right Upper Extremity: arms, shoulders, elbows, wrists, hands
  • Left / Right Lower Extremity: hips, thighs, knees, ankles, feet
  • Other Ratings: all remaining non-extremity or non-bilateral ratings
38 CFR § 4.26(d) logic used here:
1. Identify compensable bilateral candidates in paired extremities.
2. Test valid bilateral subsets for upper only, lower only, or combined upper and lower extremities.
3. Add 10% of the combined bilateral extremity value.
4. Combine excluded extremity ratings and other ratings separately.
5. Select the most favorable final result.
